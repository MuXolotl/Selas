# Roadmap

This document outlines the planned direction for Selas.

> [!Note]
> This roadmap is informational, not a fixed promise. Priorities may change based on bugs, compatibility work, and playtesting feedback.

## Design goals

- Keep darkness readable.
- Separate open-night lighting from sealed-cave darkness.
- Give the Overworld, Nether, and End distinct visual character.
- Stay client-side and configurable.
- Grow in small, stable steps.

## 0.2 — Atmosphere

Focus:

- atmosphere and fog groundwork
- stronger separation between open air and underground spaces
- weather-aware visual response
- clearer Overworld / Nether / End ambience
- early biome- and altitude-aware behavior

## 0.3 — Eye adaptation

Focus:

- dark-to-bright adaptation
- bright-to-dark adaptation
- better indoor / outdoor transitions
- exposure-style response for low-light and high-light scenes

## Later

Possible future work:

- color temperature controls
- color grading / tonal response
- saturation and low-light mood tuning
- improved atmospheric scattering ideas
- optional post-processing path
- optional shader-aware or shader-companion path
- broader lighting compatibility where practical
